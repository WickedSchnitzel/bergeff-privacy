3. Overview: No Personal Data Collected

Bergeff Digital Solutions Shopping List does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The App operates entirely offline. No internet connection, no external server, no data leaving your device.

  • Shopping lists you create (titles, items, quantities, checkmarks)
  • App settings you configure (display preferences, sort order, theme)

All data is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

Exception – Optional Voice Input: When you use the optional voice input feature (microphone button), your spoken audio is processed by Android’s built-in Speech Recognizer, which may transmit audio to Google’s servers. The App itself does not store or process any audio data. See Sections 4 and 5.

4. List Sharing Feature (Link Sharing)

The App includes an optional feature to share a shopping list with another user via a link (e.g. through a messaging app of your choice). The content of the selected list (its name and items) is encoded directly into the link itself — no data is transmitted to any server operated by us or any third party; the link is generated and processed entirely on your device.

The recipient’s device decodes the link locally to create a new list. We have no access to the link’s content, do not store it, and cannot see who you share it with. Because the link contains the full list content, you are solely responsible for choosing a trustworthy transmission channel and for deciding with whom you share it.

5. Internet Connection

The App operates fully offline for its core functionality. No network requests, no background synchronisation, no telemetry, no analytics.

Exception – Optional Voice Input: If you use the optional voice input feature, Android’s Speech Recognizer may require an internet connection to process your spoken words. This is handled by your device’s OS and Google’s speech service — not by the App. You can always enter items manually.

6. Third-Party Services

No analytics, advertising, social media SDKs, cloud storage, or payment processors are used for core functionality.

Exception – Google Speech Recognition (Android): The optional voice input feature uses Android’s built-in Speech Recognizer (typically Google). Audio may be sent to Google’s servers. Subject to Google’s Privacy Policy. The App does not store, access, or share any audio data. Voice input is entirely optional.

7. No Tracking or Profiling

No behaviour tracking, no location tracking, no device identifiers, no usage patterns, no user profiles, no cookies.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): No personal data held.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): Uninstall the App to delete all locally stored data.
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18 GDPR): Not applicable.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR): Not applicable.
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR): Not applicable.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR): Where processing is based on consent (voice input), you may withdraw it at any time by simply not using the voice input feature.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: Developer is based in Germany — competent authority: bfdi.bund.de. You may also contact the supervisory authority in your country of habitual residence.
