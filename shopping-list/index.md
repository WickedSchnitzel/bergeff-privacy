## 3. Overview: No Personal Data Collected

MiniSho: Simple Shopping List does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The App is designed to function entirely offline. The App itself does not establish an internet connection and does not require one for its core functionality. It does not communicate with any external server and does not send any information outside of your device — not to the developer, not to any third party, and not to any cloud service.

**Exception – Optional Voice Input:** The App includes an optional voice input feature. When you choose to use it (by tapping the microphone button), your spoken audio is processed by Android's built-in Speech Recognizer, which may transmit audio to Google's servers. The App itself does not store or process any audio data. See Sections 5 and 6 for details.

The only data the App creates and stores are:

- Shopping lists you create yourself (titles, items, quantities, checkmarks)
- App settings you configure (e.g. display preferences, sort order, theme)
- Alarm and notification settings you configure (scheduled times, weekdays, notification message text)

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device using the operating system's standard local storage mechanisms. It never leaves your device.

## 4. List Sharing Feature (Link Sharing)

The App includes an optional feature that allows you to share a shopping list with another user via a link (e.g. through a messaging app of your choice). When you use this feature, the content of the selected list (its name and items) is encoded directly into the link itself. No data is transmitted to any server operated by us or any third party — the link is generated and processed entirely on your device.

The recipient's device decodes the link locally to create a new list. We have no access to the content of the link, do not store it, and cannot see who you share it with or what it contains.

Because the link contains the full list content, you are solely responsible for choosing a trustworthy transmission channel (e.g. a private message) and for deciding with whom you share it.

## 5. Internet Connection

The App does not require or use an internet connection for its core functionality. The App itself does not establish an internet connection. It operates fully offline. There are no network requests, no background synchronisation, no telemetry, and no analytics of any kind.

**Exception – Optional Voice Input:** If you use the optional voice input feature, Android's built-in Speech Recognizer may require an active internet connection to process your spoken words. This is handled entirely by your device's operating system and Google's speech service — not by the App. You can always enter items manually without using voice input.

## 6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services for core functionality. The App does not use analytics, advertising networks, social media SDKs, cloud storage, or payment processors.

**Exception – Google Speech Recognition (Android):** The optional Voice Input feature uses Android's built-in Speech Recognizer. On most Android devices, this service is provided by Google and may send your spoken audio to Google's servers for processing. This may involve a transfer of data to third countries outside the EU/EEA, in particular to the United States. Google LLC is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. The App does not store, access, or share any audio data. Voice input is entirely optional — you may always type items manually.

**Legal basis (GDPR):** The processing of audio data via the voice input feature is based on your freely given consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), expressed by actively pressing and holding the microphone button. You may withdraw this consent at any time by simply not using the voice input feature.

## 7. No Tracking or Profiling

The App does not track your behaviour, location, device identifiers, or usage patterns. No user profiles are created. No cookies or similar tracking technologies are used. No automated decision-making including profiling within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR takes place.

## 8. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The only data ever stored by the App is what you explicitly enter:

- Shopping lists (titles, items) — stored on your device for core app functionality
- App settings and preferences — stored on your device for personalising the app experience
- Alarm and notification settings (scheduled times, weekdays, message text) — stored on your device for the reminder feature

You can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data via your device's system settings. The developer has no access to this data and cannot recover it on your behalf.

## 9. Storage Duration

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you uninstall the App or manually clear the App's data via your device's system settings. The developer has no access to this data and does not store it on any server.

For audio data processed by Google via the optional voice input feature, the storage duration is governed by Google LLC's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children. It is therefore suitable for use by users of all ages from a data protection standpoint. No age verification is required, as no personal data is processed at any point.

## 11. Your Rights Under Applicable Law (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Although the App does not process any personal data, the following rights are granted to you under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and equivalent national legislation. You may exercise these rights at any time by contacting us:

- **Right of access** (Art. 15 GDPR): Since we process no personal data, there is nothing to disclose.
- **Right to rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR): No personal data is held by us.
- **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten") (Art. 17 GDPR): All data resides locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.
- **Right to restriction of processing** (Art. 18 GDPR): Not applicable, as no personal data is processed.
- **Right to data portability** (Art. 20 GDPR): Not applicable, as no personal data is processed by us.
- **Right to object** (Art. 21 GDPR): Not applicable, as no personal data is processed.
- **Right to withdraw consent** (Art. 7(3) GDPR): Where processing is based on consent (voice input), you may withdraw it at any time by simply not using the voice input feature.
- **Right to lodge a complaint**: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. As the developer is based in Germany, the competent authority is the State Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information of Baden-Württemberg (LfDI BW): https://www.baden-wuerttemberg.datenschutz.de. A list of all German data protection authorities is available at: https://www.bfdi.bund.de — You may also contact the supervisory authority in your country of habitual residence. If you are in the United Kingdom, you may also contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): https://ico.org.uk.
