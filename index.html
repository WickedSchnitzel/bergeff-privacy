3. Overview: No Personal Data Collected

Wickapps Shopping List does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The App operates entirely offline. No internet connection, no external server, no data leaving your device.

  • Shopping lists you create (titles, items, quantities, checkmarks)
  • App settings you configure (display preferences, sort order, theme)

All data is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.
